# Privacy Policy

Blue Frog Analytics ("we" or "us") provides tracking and analytics services. This policy explains the information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

## Information We Collect
- **Account Information** &ndash; details you provide such as name and email when creating an account or communicating with us.
- **Usage and Log Data** &ndash; pages you visit, actions you take, IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and timestamps.
- **Tracking Technologies** &ndash; cookies, pixels, and similar tools that monitor behavior across our site and partner sites.
- **Third-Party Data** &ndash; information from advertising networks and data partners that help enhance our analytics.

## How We Use Information
- Operate, maintain, and improve our services.
- Analyze usage trends to refine features and develop new offerings.
- Personalize content, advertising, and communications.
- Detect and prevent technical or security issues.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## Sharing Your Information
- **Service Providers** &ndash; vendors assisting with analytics, hosting, marketing, and other operations.
- **Advertising Partners** &ndash; third parties that deliver targeted ads and measure campaign performance.
- **Business Transfers** &ndash; if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.
- **Legal Requirements** &ndash; when disclosure is necessary to meet the law or protect our rights.

## Cookies and Tracking
We use first&ndash; and third&ndash;party cookies, tags, and scripts to recognize you and gather information about your visit. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly without them.

## Data Retention
We retain personal data as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

## Your Choices
- Access, update, or delete your account information through your dashboard.
- Opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in those messages.
- Adjust your browser settings to decline cookies.
- Depending on your location, you may request additional rights over your data.

## Security
We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

## International Transfers
Your data may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted here or communicated through our website.

For questions about this policy or our data practices, please [contact us](/contact/).
