# Your Privacy Choices

Blue Frog Analytics gives you control over your personal information. The options below help you manage how data is collected and used:

- **Cookie Preferences** &ndash; Adjust your browser or device settings to control cookies and tracking technologies.
- **Email Communications** &ndash; Use the unsubscribe link in our emails or update your preferences in the dashboard.
- **Data Requests** &ndash; You may request access to or deletion of your data by [contacting us](/contact/).

For more details about our practices, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).
